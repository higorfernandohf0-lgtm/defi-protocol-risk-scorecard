# DeFi Protocol Risk Scorecard

This report compares DeFi protocols using public risk indicators.

## Methodology

Each protocol receives a score from 0 to 100 based on:

- TVL Score: 25 points
- TVL Stability: 20 points
- Protocol Age: 20 points
- Holder Concentration: 20 points
- Audit / Exploit History: 15 points

## Risk Levels

- 80–100: Low Risk
- 60–79: Medium Risk
- 0–59: High Risk

## Key Insights

- All selected protocols scored as Low Risk because they are large, mature DeFi protocols.
- Aave achieved the highest score due to strong TVL, stability, and maturity.
- Curve had the lowest score among the group, mainly due to lower TVL compared with the others.
- This MVP uses public indicators and manual inputs for holder concentration and audit/exploit history.
